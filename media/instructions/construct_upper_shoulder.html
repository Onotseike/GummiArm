Upper shoulder

Required :

  1. 1x 120 mm Dynamixel 3 pins cable
  2. 1x 100 mm Dynamixel 3 pins cable
  3. 1x 140mm slewing bearing
  4. 2x Dynamixel Hub
  5. 1x SMPS2Dynamixel
  6. 40x M2x8mm screws
  7. 32x M2 washers
  8. 32x M2 nuts
  9. 4x M2.5x10mm screws
  10. 28x M2.5x12mm screws
  11. 70x M2.5x8mm screws
  12. 85x M2.5 washers
  13. 56x M2.5 nuts
  14. 1x M3x10mm with bearing bushing
  15. 12x M3x20mm screws
  16. 2x M3x50mm screws
  17. 22x M3 washers
  18. 14x M3 nuts

1. Screw the exterior part of the 140mm slewnig bearing to the shoulder roll support with M3x20 mm screws. (Figure 1)
2. Fix the the flexion-extension encoder part to the servo 1 (M2x8mm). (Figure 2)
3. Put one Dynamixel Hub on each side of the back shoulder (M2x8mm) and fix one support encoder servo (M2.5x12mm). (Figure 3)
4. Screw the Dynamixel AX-12A with M2x8mm screws. (Figure 4) 5. Put four M2.5 nuts in the foreseen hole behind the exterior roll plate and fix the other support encoder (M2.5x12mm). (Figure 5)
6. Turn back this last part and fix the servos (2 and 3) in the foreseen holes (M2.5x8mm). Make corresponding holes of the slewing bearing with muscles support ring and exterior roll plate and fix them together (M3x20mm). Fix then The shoulder muscles previously prepared to ther servos (M2.5x12mm). (Figure 6)
7. Put servos 4 and 5 on the shoulder tray and screw it afer having connected the two. Screw then the part prepared in step 6 to the shoulder tray and to the servos with M2.5x8 and M2.5x12 mm. (Figure 7)
8. Put the assembled back_shoulder and fix it to the servos with M2.5x8mm. Put then the SMPS2Dynamixelto this part. (Figure 8 and 8.5)
9. Fix first the Dynamixel MX-28 to the support clavicle and fix on this support the two clavicle parts with M2.5x8 and M2.5x12 mm (Figure 9 and 9.5)
10. Fix to the two axes (M3x50mm) the muscles and nylon fibers (Figure 10)
11. Screw the servo 11 both side and using the bearing bushing for one. (Figure 11 and 11.5)
12. Fix the assembled clavicle to the shoulder with M2x8mm (Figure 12) 13. Put one medium wheel for each clavicle muscles and fix them to the corresponding servo. (Figure 13)
14. Cross the two big muscles around the ring and fix them to each servo (4 and 5). (Figure 14)
