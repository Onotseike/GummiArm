Head

Required :

  1. 1x 180 mm dynamixel 3 pin cable
  2. 1x 120mm Dynamixel Hub
  3. 1x slewing bearing
  4. 24x M2x8mm screws
  5. 24x M2 washers
  6. 20x M2 nuts
  7. 15x M3x20mm screws
  8. 30x M3 washers
  9. 15x M3 nuts
  10. 2x M6x12 mm screws
  11. 2x M6 washers

1. Fit FilaFlex orange parts in kinect shelf (see Figure 1).
2. Fix servo 101 with 16 M2 x 8 mm screws and corresponding washers (Figure 2)
3. Screw the kinect and the shelf together through the slewing bearing, with M3 screws (Figure 3)
4. Screw the slewing bearing to your structure 9M3 screws) and fix (with M6 x 12 mm) the rotating head part to your structure once you have screwed the encoder with it. And finally fix a Dynamixel hub to the last part. (Figure 4)
