Lower shoulder

Required :

  1. underneath muscles
  2. 3x 100 mm Dynamixel 3 pins cable
  3. 1x 200 mm Dynamixel 3 pins cable
  4. 1x 140mm slewing bearing
  5. 1x Dynamixel Hub
  6. 24x M2x8mm screws
  7. 20x M2 washers
  8. 20x M2 nuts
  9. 12x M2.5x12mm screws
  10. 8x M2.5x8mm screws
  11. 12x M2.5 washers
  12. 10x M2.5 nuts
  13. 10x M3x20mm screws
  14. 20x M3 washers
  15. 10x M3 nuts
  16. 6x M6x12 mm screws
  17. 6x M6 washers

1. Put 4 M2.5 nuts in the shoulder tray (see Figure 1).
2. Fix the Dynamixel Hub inside the underside plate. Plug on the hub all the cables. (Figure 2)
3. Plug the cables to ther Servos 2 (on the right) and 3 (on the left) with 100mm cables. (Figure 3)
4. Screws the 2 MX-106 to the undersideplate (M2.5x12mm) and the encoder number 8 (m2x8mm) and plug the third 100mm cable to this encoder. (Figure 4) 5. Screw the shoulder tray and the underside plate together through the 140mm slewing bearing, and fix the bearing to the structure. (Figure 5)
6. Fix the previously prepared wheels (Figure 6)
7. Fix to the structure the two support structure, put the filaflex and the nylon between that and the muscles fixer. Try to have the nylon surrounding the M6 screw to stuck the wire. Try to align the muscles with the wheels to avoid any damages on the wheel. Then fix like previously the rotating shoulder part to the structure and to the servo. (Figure 7)
