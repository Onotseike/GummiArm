Cost to build an GummiArm

So how much cost building the GummiArm? Since most of its part are 3D printed, this soft robot arm is quiet cheap and if you break it during experiments or other projects you can easily print damaged parts.

Here is a recap chart containing all information about component's prices (on 09/23/2017). All the prices below are on an indicative basis, you may use other equivalent part than the one we used.
