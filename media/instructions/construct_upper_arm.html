Upper arm

Required :

  1. 1x 100mm Dynamixel 3 pins cable
  2. 1x Dynamixel Hub
  3. 20x M2x8mm screws
  4. 12x M2 washers
  5. 16x M2 nuts
  6. 6x M2.5x12mm screws
  7. 44x M2.5x8mm screws
  8. 36x M2.5 nuts
  9. 45x M2.5 washers
  10. 1x M3x10mm with bearing bushing
  11. 2x M3x50mm screws
  12. 4x M3 washers
  13. 2x M3 nuts

    14. 1. Use M3x50 mm as axes again to fix the tendons thank to the muscles and nylon holders and lock them in wheels. Then fix servo 15(Figure 1 and 1.5)
    2. Fix the upper plate underneath the servo 12 and put the hub support on the side of this servo. (Figure 2)
    3. After having connected servos 13 and 14, screw a biceps-triceps reinforcement plastic part on each side. Then screw the lower plate on the servo 14. (Figure 3)
    4. Fix the servo 15 to the elbow encoder support, and put a wheel on each servo (biceps on servo 13 and triceps on servo 14). (Figure 4)
